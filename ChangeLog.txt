Key Changes in Version 1.2.5:

    Updated Color Scheme:
        5 μg/m³: #63BAE4 (Light Blue)
        15 μg/m³: #30C934 (Light Green)
        25 μg/m³: #ECD93B (Yellow)
        35 μg/m³: #F9952B (Orange)
        45 μg/m³: #F81334 (Red)
        55 μg/m³: #C411B9 (Purple)
        65+ μg/m³: #79157A (Dark Purple)

    Dynamic Breathing Patterns:
        45-55 μg/m³: 5-second breathing cycle
        55-65 μg/m³: 3-second breathing cycle
        65+ μg/m³: 2-second breathing cycle

    Updated Web Interface:
        New gradient bar with all 7 colors
        Updated debug information with new breathing cycle labels
        Extended AQI scale up to 65+ μg/m³

    Version Update: Changed from 1.2.4 to 1.2.5

The breathing effect now becomes faster and more intense as air pollution levels increase, providing a more urgent visual warning for dangerous air quality conditions.
